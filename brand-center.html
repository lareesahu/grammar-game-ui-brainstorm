Brand Identity

Core brand elements — locked as of 2026-07-19

Name

Gramlingo — a portmanteau of "Grammar" + "Lingo" (slang for language), also plays on "Gramlin-Go!" — the grammar gremlin on a quest.

Tagline

"Grammar Quest — Learn by playing."
A systemic grammar game. Modular, progressive, playful. For Chinese English learners.

Domain

gramlingo.com — available ✓
Live at lareesahu.github.io/gramlingo

Character

Gramlin — a small, round, mint-green grammar gremlin. Big curious eyes, tiny curved horns, juggles punctuation marks. Pokémon meets Studio Ghibli soot sprite.
