🧌 Gramlingo — Brand Identity System

Gramlin-Go! · The grammar gremlin. 5 options × 7 categories. ★ marks top pick.

1. Icon — 5 Concepts

The mascot / app icon / favicon. Must work at 16×16px.

🧌 Round Gramlin Face

🧌
Round creature, big shiny eyes, tiny horns, slight grin. Feels like Pokémon × Duolingo. Works at all sizes. Universal appeal.

📝 Gramlin Holding Pencil

✏️🧌
Small gremlin clutching an oversized pencil — "writing the rules." Instantly signals grammar + learning. Too detailed at favicon size but great for hero/branding.

◉ G Monogram

◉
A circle with a tail forming a "G" — clean, professional. Reads at 16px. The Gramlin face can be a secondary mascot, this is the primary mark.

🦜 Parrot with Quote Bubble

🦜💬
Parrot = repetition/mimicry (grammar practice). Speech bubble = language. Fun but less unique — many language apps use birds.

⬡ Sentence Hexagon

⬡
Hexagonal crystal with a "G" carved into it. Systemic, modular vibes. Good for the game engine identity — each module is a hex tile.

Gramlingo

3. Wordmark Letter Kerning — 5 Treatments

Spacing between individual letter pairs.

Gram·lingo — split kerning

Gram·lingo
Tighter on "Gram" (-0.04em), looser on "lingo" (0.06em). A subtle dot or dot between them. Emphasizes the wordplay: Gram + lingo.

GR A M L I N G O — wide

GRAMLINGO
Spaced-out caps. Luxury/premium vibe. Good for hero lockups. Loses the "cute" factor — feels like an industrial brand.

gramlingo — tight lowercase

gramlingo
All lowercase, tight spacing. Modern SaaS vibe. Feels approachable. Good for the app icon overlay. Not distinctive enough alone.

Graml·ingo — mid-word dot

Graml·ingo
A center dot at the syllable break. Clean, European typography feel. Good for the wordmark but may confuse non-designers.

G r a m l i n g o — tracking only

Gramlingo
Even tracking at 0.15em. Simple, no tricks. Most accessible. Doesn't emphasize the wordplay but reads cleanest in small sizes.

4. Logo Letter Spacing — 5 Values

Overall tracking for the complete logo mark.

+0.04em — snug default

Gramlingo
Best balance. Letters breathe but stay connected. Works at ALL sizes — favicon to hero. Zero distortion.

−0.02em — tight display

Gramlingo
Great for large hero sizes. Letters touch slightly — feels premium. Breaks down below 24px.

+0.10em — airy

Gramlingo
Breathable, modern. Good for the nav bar logo. Too loose for the primary mark — loses cohesion.

0 — default font spacing

Gramlingo
Whatever the font ships with. Requires no adjustment. Slightly inconsistent between letters with a playful font like Baloo 2.

+0.02em caps, −0.03em lowercase

GRAMlingo
Dual tracking: "GRAM" spaced out, "lingo" tucked in. Emphasizes the name structure. Experimental — love it or hate it.

6. Brand Typography Combo — 5 Pairs

Heading font + body font. Must work on Web (Google Fonts) and iOS/Android.

Baloo 2 + Nunito

Relative Clause Master
This is body copy. The quick brown fox. 适合中国英语学习者。Clear, rounded, friendly — reads well in both EN and ZH.
Both rounded, both Google Fonts. Baloo 2 handles display; Nunito handles long-form. CONSISTENT personality across sizes.

Fredoka One + Inter

Relative Clause Master
This is body copy. The quick brown fox. Inter is the industry standard for body — neutral, lets Fredoka shine.
Playful heading + utilitarian body. Good contrast. Inter has excellent ZH support. Most professional of the playful combos.

Playfair Display + Lora

Relative Clause Master
This is body copy. Elegant, editorial. The Gramatica / Syntaxia direction. Too serious for the Gramlin brand.
Serif + serif. Premium editorial. Wrong vibe for "adorable grammar gremlin." Excellent for a textbook/IELTS spinoff.

Poppins + DM Sans

Relative Clause Master
This is body copy. Geometric + geometric. Clean, modern, slightly corporate. Good for admin/dashboard screens.
Geometric pair. Most "SaaS" feel. Good for the dashboard/instructor view. Poppins is also a great logo font alternate.

Comic Neue + Atkinson Hyperlegible

Relative Clause Master
This is body copy. Maximum accessibility. Comic Neue keeps the playful Gramlin spirit. Atkinson is designed for readability.
Accessibility-first. Comic Neue = playful handwriting without Comic Sans shame. Atkinson = Braille Institute's legibility font.
